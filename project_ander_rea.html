Ander Rea

The trap, rap, and reggaeton artist Ander Rea reached out to Rodrygo Design for the creation of a music video and cover art for his new rap single, "Welcome Project 8". The project required a unique and creative approach to align with the artist’s vision for the song, blending raw authenticity with bold, innovative visuals.

Design Rationale

For the music video, we traveled to a small village in Cazorla, where we recorded a "one-shot" sequence inside a house, carefully following the client’s specific requests. In post-production, the video was edited to include a night vision effect, as requested by Ander Rea, and enhanced with additional visual and sound elements to elevate the final result. For the cover art, we organized a photoshoot with the artist and meticulously edited the selected images, ensuring the final design matched his detailed specifications and perfectly complemented the theme of "Welcome Project 8".
