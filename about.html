¡Welcome!

I am Rodrigo Seco Fiastre, a spanish certified graphic designer with expertise in a wide range of creative disciplines. These include Brand Creation, Audio-Visual Design, 3D model, Renders, Motion Graphics, Visual Identity, and Editorial Design. Additionally, I specialize in managing and designing content for social media, as well as in web development and design using HTML and CSS.

I was born in Murcia on December 27, 2001. I spent 10 years living in Madrid before moving to Alicante at the age of 16, where I completed my artistic baccalaureate at the IES El Palmeral high school in Orihuela. Afterward, I pursued a higher degree in graphic design at the Escuela de Arte Superior y Diseño de Orihuela (EASDO).

Upon completing my education, I began my professional career working as a freelance designer for various clients, as well as collaborating with the digital marketing agency Nuevecomanueve (NCN) in Orihuela.
