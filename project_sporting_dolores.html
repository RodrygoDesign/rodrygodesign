Sporting Dolores Futbol Club

Sporting Dolores Football Club reached out to Rodrygo Design to create a new redesign of the club’s emblem. The project involved transforming the old circular logo into a heraldic-style shield, giving it a more modern and professional appearance. The redesigned crest features a symmetrical shape with curved and pointed edges, narrowing elegantly at the bottom, an iconic design commonly seen in football club emblems.

Design Rationale

A unique addition to the new design is an artichoke placed at the center, symbolizing the heart of every player, as the crest is positioned on the left side of the chest. The artichoke also pays homage to its cultural significance in the local area. Furthermore, the crest now proudly displays the club’s founding year, "2019," integrated into the design. Black, white, and red were chosen as the primary colors to align with the team’s kit, an element the previous logo overlooked. This redesign captures both the club’s identity and its connection to the community while embracing a bold, contemporary aesthetic.
